Summary:

India sees a vast difference in literacy rate across states, (~30% diff. between max and min)

This project will explore how different factors might/might not affect these literacy rates.

They are:

  • Accessibility through all-weather roads.
  • Number of schools / population
  • Male v/s Female admissions in school
  • SC/ST/OBC students and whether they go to private/public schools.
